Work case study

Jalepinos Mexican bar & Grill

Jalepinos is a mexican restaurant that had a less than perfect logo and just over all identity problems. I redesigned their logo and made mock ups of how the logo would look on various items in their restaurant.



This would have been Jalepinos new work shirt. It displays their new logo in many different places to make a clean looking work shirt.

I took their newly designed logo and incorporated it in many ways. One of the ways would have been on their carry out containers. This one displays a coffee cup.

Keeping with their newly designed logo I designed some business cards for them as well. I thought it would be a good idea to have a yellow businesscar with their logo displayed on the top left. The yellow of the business card really complimented the colors in their logo.
